Before You Enter
Community Chat — Read & Agree

1. Social community only — Entertainment and poker community discussion. No real money transactions.

2. You must be 18+ to participate. By agreeing you confirm this.

3. No illegal activity — No cheating, collusion, chip dumping, or illegal gambling discussion.

4. No harassment — Respect all participants. No hate speech, threats, or abuse.

5. Chat is monitored and logged — All messages (including private and secret channels) are recorded with timestamps and session data. The moderator can review all logs at any time.

6. No spam or advertising — No unsolicited links or promos.

7. Moderator rights — Dhezz may remove messages or ban users at any time.

8. Private channels — Private/secret channels are hidden from other users but are still fully logged and visible to the moderator.

9. Continued use = acceptance. Terms may update at any time.
